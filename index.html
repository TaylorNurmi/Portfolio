A little about me and why I love being a Developer

I am a Software Developer who has had a passion for software since I was a child. It began with gaming and then a desire to figure out how all if it works. One of my close friends is a Python Developer and he began showing me some of his creations and I was hooked. I began self-teaching a few years ago with various applications. My favorites being Codecademy and SoloLearn, I decided I wanted to take it to the next level so I attended Coding Dojo.

While attending the Dojo I began building Full Stack Application in Python with both Flask and Django. I have quit a few tools on my belt now and adding more nearly daily. It feels like a superpower as I can create almost anything I can imagine. My favorite languages are Python and JavaScript. I am incredibly grateful to Coding Dojo for teaching strong fundamentals of programming, this makes it so much easier to pick up any language or framework and learn it quickly.
